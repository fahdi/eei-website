FAQs

What type of positions do you handle?

EE International was established in Dubai to suit the specialty needs for both Employers and Employees in the MENA region, and have become a leading boutique search practice for design consultancy, project management, construction management, and construction firms, and across a wide variety of specialty industries for part time, full time, and contract positions.

What types of recruitment arrangements does EE International provide?

Depending on the employer's needs, we will work on a contingency or a retainer basis. We will conduct a needs analysis to determine the best arrangement for your company.

Do you meet with your candidates in person?

Yes, we make it a point to meet with our candidates in person whenever possible to evaluate their overall presentation, skill set, professional demeanor and personality before we schedule an interview with your company. Depending on the location of the job, there are occasions that we may arrange to skype our candidate and or arrange an in-depth phone interview. However, we will always inform your company if an in person interview did not take place.

Do you check references on your candidates before you schedule an interview?

Yes, we will not schedule a company interview with our candidate until we have conducted a thorough reference check. The last thing we want to do is to waste your time and send you candidates that you are serious about hiring, only to receive a bad reference after the fact.

My internal recruiter is going out on leave. Can EE International come in and fill in during that time?

Most definitely. We have experienced consultants on site for clients on both a long and short-term basis, or we can place a qualified recruiter on an interim basis.

I have recruitment needs outside the MENA region and in other disciplines noted in your description of services, can EE International assist me?

Absolutely. We have the expertise, and know how to fill any position outside our specialty areas, as long as we have a clear understanding of your needs. We would schedule an depth conversation with your hiring authority to learn the requirements, and qualifications needed to create a longstanding and successful fit. Once the needs analysis has been conducted, we task our research team and account managers to identify, locate, and qualify the best prospects for your open job opportunities.

What is the process to get started?

First, we discuss your recruitment needs, what you have tried in the past, and what challenges have you met along the way. Next, we will create a proposal around that conversation as well as provide you with recruitment solutions to help streamline your process. Finally, one of our associates will visit your site to meet with your recruiting team and hiring managers, to get a solid feel for your culture and specific needs.

What is the time frame between initial discussion and start date of new hire?

The time frame is dependent upon your candidate qualifications and job requirements, availability, internal and authority processes.

Privacy Statement

Information Gathering and Use

Personal information acquired through candidate registration – such as candidate’s name, address, telephone number e-mail address, and CV is gathered to provide EEI with the data needed to determine whether a candidate is an appropriate match for available positions with our clients and promote a candidate.

When a candidate logs into the EEI system, creates a profile, and uploads their information and CV, the same is stored in the EEI database. Candidate information remains in our database until removed by the candidate, or if the candidate becomes unavailable as a result of being hired by one of our clients. Candidates may also revise and/or update their information at any time.

Who Receives Candidate Information

Once our clients request specific staff for their operations, EEI executive search recruiters filter the EEI database and shortlist candidates with profiles that match our client’s requirements. EEI executive search recruiters then analyze the data received from candidates in further detail to determine which candidates are best suited for the position/s desired by our clients, and forward the CV s of the best qualified and suitable candidates to our clients for their review and approval.

EEI is dedicated to building relationships with our clients and candidates. Candidate information will not be shared with other recruiters or organizations outside EEI other than our clients requesting specific staff. Any information provided through EEI is strictly confidential between our clients and candidates. Candidate information is intended for the sole use of EEI working toward placement of candidates with our clients.
